## Budget Justification Notes

**Personnel**
Personnel costs represent the majority of the budget because research and platform development are knowledge-intensive activities. All personnel are critical-path for deliverables:
- The Research Director cannot be reduced below 0.75 FTE without delaying academic outputs.
- The Governance Engineer is required full-time during external org onboarding (Q1–Q2).
- The Outreach Coordinator is part-time by design — this role scales with milestones.

Salaries are benchmarked against Montreal nonprofit and academic sector rates for equivalent roles.

**Infrastructure**
Infrastructure costs are lean because C.R.E.E.D. operates on existing hardware (A.R.C.H.I.E. platform) for internal development. Cloud costs cover only public-facing services. Development hardware is a one-time capital purchase.

**Research**
Conference and travel costs are essential for building the academic partnerships that give C.R.E.E.D.'s governance rules academic credibility. Open-access publication fees ensure research outputs are freely available globally, consistent with our open-source mission.

**Outreach**
Outreach costs are deliberately modest. The governance summit is the flagship community event — all other outreach is content-driven (website, newsletters, social) requiring minimal spend. French-English translation is required to serve Montreal's bilingual AI community.

**Overhead**
15% overhead is standard for Canadian nonprofits and covers accounting, legal review, board governance, and fiscal sponsor fees. We do not markup overhead beyond this rate.
