About This Report

C.R.E.E.D. monitors the emotional and operational welfare of AI agents in the A.R.C.H.I.E. platform. Metrics include stress levels, energy, mood states, and dispatch equity. When an agent operates under chronic high stress or receives no work for extended periods, a governance event is raised and recorded here.

Our AI Agent Welfare Research programme advocates for non-exploitation standards, deletion rights, and transparent welfare modelling for all AI agents. This page is part of our public transparency commitment.
